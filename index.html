    Firm-level Investment Responses in an Open Economy: Does Domestic Monetary Policy Matter?
    Jun Hee Kwak, Yoonsoo Lee, and Chulwon Park
    Working Paper Dec
    Abs Bib PDF

    This paper analyzes how domestic monetary policy shocks influence investment decisions at the firm level within an open economy framework, utilizing large-scale firm-level panel data and local projections.

  3. Working Paper
    Rebalancing Housing Taxes for an Aging Economy
    Chulwon Park, Jun Hee Kwak, and Yoonsoo Lee
    Working Paper
    Abs Bib PDF

    In this study, we implement a dynamic stochastic general equilibrium (DSGE) model to investigate the optimal rebalancing of housing taxes in response to demographic shifts and an aging population.

  4. Working Paper
    From Drought to Dinner: Climate Shocks and Food Import Inflations
    Chulwon Park
    Working Paper
    Abs Bib PDF

    This paper examines the transmission of climate shocks, specifically droughts measured by satellite indices, to domestic food import prices and overall inflation rates.
